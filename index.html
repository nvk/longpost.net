Welcome to LongPost.net

A simple tool to create and edit NIP-23 long-form notes on Nostr.

Use your NIP-07 wallet/extension to sign in, paste Markdown, preview, and publish. Automatically pulls your relays (if supported) and publishes your content via Nostr.
